What is Mentor Shala?

This is new web based mentoring system that is to be developed for mentoring students from all domains, mainly for students that are having lack of guidance in career and need clearance in choosing subject streams or career path for better opportunities. Also this system will help skilled students (mentors) from higher standards and good schools or colleges having better understanding and experiences to share their learnings with the students who need guidance in respective domains.Read more
